Starting Out

So you've decided to make a change in your life. You've ignored your body for too long. Too many hours in front of a screen. The time is now before it's too late! It's time to get fit! But where to start? The gym is an expensive commitment and just isn't right. You want to ease yourself into a happier and healthier lifestyle.

Get Off The Couch

In the UK the National Health Service runs a program known as Couch To 5K. It helps people like us take control of our bodies and sets out a fantastic program for even the most unhealthy people to get up and acheive. There is a very useful podcast which give you a personal trainer in your earphones talking you through the steps week by week.

Less Annoying

If you're anything like me you would prefer to not be distracted with boyband sugar-coated pop music or the scraping voice of the commentator. You just want to know when to run and when to walk. RunTunes has been created to give you just that and lets you ease yourself into a healthier life without having to listen to tortuous pop music.

Simple Instructions

RunTunes has been created to be as simple and universal as possible. There are just two modes: Running and not running. All you need to remember is one thing: 

When the drums are drumming, you should be running. 

But here's some other tips:

  • When the drums stop make sure you're walking
  • Try to do three runs a week
  • Good shoes help
  • Go slowly!
  • Do some stretching after each run
  • Don't over do it!
