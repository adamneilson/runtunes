Starting Out

So you've decided to make a change in your life. You've ignored it for too long. The time is now!

Less Annoying

There are already many great downloads already available for 5K training schedules but each and every one has cheesy music and commentary that distracts you from running. RunTunes has been created to let you ease into a healthier life without having to listen to tortuous music. 

Simple Instructions

RunTunes has been created to be as simple and universal as possible. There are just two modes: Running and not running. All you need to remember is: When the drums are drumming, you should be running. When they're not you should be walking.
